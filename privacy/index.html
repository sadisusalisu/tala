Privacy Policy for TALA

Effective Date: July 2, 2025

Developer: Sateplus Ltd

Contact Email: sadisusalisu@gmail.com

1. Introduction

TALA is a storytelling app for children that provides Hausa and English audio stories. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

2. What Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

  • Email Address (only if you choose to register or log in)

b. Non-Personal Information

  • App usage frequency
  • Story categories accessed
  • Favorites saved (stored locally)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide features such as saving favorites, story playback, and content recommendations.

4. Data Storage & Security

All user data is securely stored using Supabase. No sensitive data is shared with third parties. Offline audio and favorites are stored locally on your device.

5. Children’s Privacy

TALA is designed for children. We comply with COPPA principles. No ads or tracking technologies are used.

6. Permissions

  • Internet – to download stories and audio files
  • Storage – to save audio for offline playback
  • Microphone – (optional, for future features only)

7. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase, audioplayers, and other open-source Flutter packages only. No ads or external trackers are used.

8. Your Privacy Choices

  • You can use the app without creating an account
  • Uninstalling the app deletes all local data
  • You may request deletion of account data via email

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. Updates will be posted here with a new effective date.

10. Contact

For any questions, email: sadisusalisu@gmail.com
